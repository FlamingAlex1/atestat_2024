How to determine skin type

Oily skin
Dry skin
Combination skin
Characteristics:
Excess sebum production. Enlarged pores. Prone to acne and blackheads. Shiny complexion.

How to Identify:
Skin appears shiny, especially in the T-zone (forehead, nose, and chin). Frequent acne breakouts or blackheads. Pores may appear larger.
Characteristics:
Insufficient sebum production. Tightness and rough texture. Dull complexion. Fine lines and flakiness.

How to Identify:
Skin feels tight, especially after cleansing. Noticeable dry patches or flakiness. Prone to redness and irritation.
Characteristics:
Different areas exhibit different characteristics (e.g., oily T-zone and dry cheeks). Pores may be larger in the T-zone. Prone to both acne and dryness.

How to Identify:
Oily or shiny forehead, nose, and chin. Dry or normal cheeks and eye areas. May require a combination of skincare products.
